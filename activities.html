Things to do

  • Kirkland Arts Center — A vibrant gallery showcasing local art, classes, and workshops. 620 Market St, Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Kirkland Performance Center — Hosts live plays, concerts, comedy, and community performances. 350 Kirkland Ave, Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Totem Lake Village — A shopping and dining hub with entertainment options for all ages. 11919 NE Totem Lake Blvd, Kirkland, WA 98034
  • Peter Kirk Community Center — Offers community events, indoor recreation, and classes. 352 Kirkland Ave, Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Cross Kirkland Corridor Trail — Scenic trail for walking, biking, and enjoying public art along the way. 740 17th Ave, Downtown Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Bridle Trails State Park — Trails through forests perfect for hiking and horseback riding. 5300 124th Ave NE, Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Totem Lake Trails — Walking paths with nature views and peaceful scenery. Totem Lake Blvd Access, Kirkland, WA 98034
  • Lake Washington Sailing Club — Watch or learn sailing on Lake Washington. 269 Lake St S, Kirkland, WA 98033
  • Kirkland Mini Golf & Entertainment — Fun indoor mini golf and arcade activities. 123 Main St, Kirkland, WA 98033

Discover Parks

Marina Park features a sandy beach, boat launch, public art and a beautiful open-air pavilion as well as summer concerts and magnificent views. 25 Lakeshore Plaza Drive, Kirkland 98033

Juanita Bay Park features flora, fauna, views of Forbes Creek Wetland, Juanita Beach, and Juanita Bay. 2201 Market St, Kirkland 98033

McAuliffe Park is located on a historic farm site in Kirkland's Juanita neighborhood. 10824 NE 116 Street, Kirkland 98034

A waterfront community park located in Kirkland's Lakeview neighborhood. 5811 Lake Washington Blvd, Kirkland 98033

Everest Park is a community park with many amenities, including pickleball courts, ballfields, a playground, and a picnic shelter. 500 8th St S, Kirkland 98033
